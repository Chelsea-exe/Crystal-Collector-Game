The Crystal Collector

Game Summary

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. Click on the crystal, a specific amount of point will be added to your score You win the game by matching your score to the random number. You lose if your score is more than the random number. The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it. When the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
